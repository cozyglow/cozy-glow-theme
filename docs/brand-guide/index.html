Brand Guide

The Cozy Glow brand is more than just a logo. It is a visual entity made up of many elements that work together to convey the core of what Cozy Glow is and what we stand for.

The goal of this brand guide is to establish a clear, consistent and unique visual entity for Cozy Glow. Uniform graphics and messages provide the framework for establishing a visual entity.

This guide sets out the prime elements required to produce approved Cozy Glow marketing and communications material.
